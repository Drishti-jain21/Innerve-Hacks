About Yoga/Therapy Classes

These paid classes(nominal price for underprivileged)have been designed to help pregnant and postpartum women improve their mental as well as physical health.

Process

How it Works

  • 01

    Our webapp will assign a doctor and a helper during the postpartum duration.

  • 02

    Following checking will be madeFirst visit - If the delivery has not happened in the health centre, the first visit is to be within 24 hours of delivery.

    • Second visit - 3rd day after delivery
    • Third visit - 7th day after delivery
    • Fourth visit - 6 weeks after delivery

  • 03

    A checkup for PPD(postpartum depression) will be also done

  • 04

    Therapic sessions will be conducted
